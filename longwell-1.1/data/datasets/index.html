RDF Data Collection

This is the collection of RDF datasets that is produced and/or consumed by the SIMILE project.

Redistributable

  • Some of the MIT OpenCourseWare Material RDF-ized by the SIMILE Team (ontologies used are unofficial, the cwspace.mit.edu project will create the official data dumps out of OCW for digital preservation purposes) [see the Official Site]
  • Library of Congress' Thesaurus of Graphic Materials RDF-ized by the SIMILE team [see the official site]
  • CIA's World Factbook [see the official site]
  • Harvard University's Visual Information Access digital art collection [see the official site]
  • Princeton University's WordNet, a lexical database for the english language [see the official site]
  • Web Consortium's Technical Reports [see the official site]
  • Web Consortium's Glossary [see the official site]
  • Hewlett Packard's Research Labs Technical Reports [see the official site]
  • Some Friend of a Friend data [see the official site]
  • SIMILE's Bibliography
  • SIMILE's Glossary
  • SIMILE Longwell's class dependencies encoded as RDF
  • The National Cancer Institute's cancer thesaurus as an OWL ontology
